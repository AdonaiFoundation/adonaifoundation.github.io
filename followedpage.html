Projects and Initiatives Executed

1. Table of Men Football Gala: I began this project in 2013 in my school, at a time I was a boarding house tutor to keep the boarding house lit with excitement every weekend. Table of Men offered students the unique opportunity of simulating what happens in a real world of football, i.e. buying players; transfers; awarding best manager and players; sacking and replacing managers; sanctioning unruly players etc. The currency of transactions was also generated by some students to monitor and promote financial fair play. Table of Men is still the most exciting thing to look out for every weekend.

2. Students’ Court System: I also engineered a system to curb students bullying and at the same time promote legal appreciation in the boarding house. Students applied for the bar license, which enables them to represent clients (students), there were court marshals who saw to the latter the ruling of the judge (me). Students who were sentenced faced sanctions such as 2 days’ community service, 15 mins detention, toilet scrubbing etc.

3. Students Summer Volunteering: This project began in 2017 to help promote aquatic literacy across the various communities in Kumasi, where students of my swim school go to schools to educate them of drowning prevention strategies, raise money to sponsor learn-to-swim for the marginalized and teach other kids swimming.

4. African Sharks Swim team: This is one of my ongoing projects in which I scout swimmers with talents, groom them and take them for swimming competitions. This started in September 2018 and have featured in 6 different national and invitational swimming competitions. In total we garnered, 11 medals out of which two are gold.

5. Aquatic Safety Education Program ASEP: This is a school skills-based water safety activity. The goal is to expose students to water safety practices, first aid education, rescue skills and survival skills when they find themselves in a precarious situation.

6. Community Anti-Drowning Literacy CALP: Project: this is a research and policy driven drowning prevention advocacy program. We support policy directions towards drowning prevention approaches with data and statistics.
