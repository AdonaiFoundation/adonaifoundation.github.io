MISSION STATEMENT

Firstly

To provide college support systems such as scholarship search, school application systems, standardized testing, and career counselling.

Secondly

To help families transition into different societies through relocation programs.

Thirdly

To provide professional short courses, certification and re-certification support for professionals and businesses

Lastly

To provide local and international platforms for collaboration, exchange, workshops, and seminars which will continue to enhance professional practice and deepen networking connections.

Vision Statement

To provide platform for professional, personal and, career development.

About US

A E L C

Adonai Foundation LBG is a not-for-profit organization registered with the focus on enhancing students’ academic experience, providing mentorship and career opportunities for them, and providing platform for assisted learning, as well as supporting professionals, families and anyone in areas such as education, professional development, career development, and traveling assistance.

Adonai Foundation LBG is founded on the principle that everyone deserves a fair chance at life, when they have opportunity.

Adonai Foundation LBG has created different platforms and projects to support professionals, students, government officials and many others, in the following areas;

• Adonai Education Program: This is the platform for students’ educational support activities, such as College Counselling and School Selection; School Application Support; Standardized Testing;

• Adonai Resettlement & Lifestyle Program: This is the platform given to professionals, families, and anyone who wants to resettle in another country. This platform helps educate, enlighten, and where possible, provide support for the settlers in the new country. Adonai Resettlement & Lifestyle program helps families transition into new lifestyles easier and smoother.

• Adonai Professional Suit: Platform where short professional courses are organized for professionals and people in business to improve, sharpen and upgrade their skills and practice for impact.

• Adonai Exchange Program: Collaborative platforms for sharing, learning, and exchanges

• Adonai Empowerment Program: Our empowerment program seeks to assemble great minds and visionaries, to impact professionals, students and organizations through seminars, webinars, and, workshops.
